Beyond pills, we deliver life ---- In just 10 minutes

Medicine on minutes is the promise of Mom Pharmacy, a dynamic startup delivering essential medicines to your doorstep in just 10 minutes. Founded in Bengaluru, Karnataka, this early-stage company began its journey in 2025 and has quickly made strides in the healthcare delivery space. With a dedicated team of 11-50 members skilled in Java full-stack development, Mom Pharmacy leverages technology to ensure swift and reliable service. Recently, the company shifted its office to the bustling hub of HiTech City, Hyderabad, marking a new chapter in its growth. Still in its early development phase, Mom Pharmacy is committed to revolutionizing access to medicines with speed and efficiency.
